Grade 8 “Hard Rock Blues” mix

This production is put together by the Grade 8 music class. They recorded a piece called “Hard Rock Blues,” based on the twelve bar blues often found in jazz. This is a band piece that they are quite familiar with. The technique of recording requires a lot of control over the instrument in order to stay in time while playing together. For this project, students each recorded the melody over a prepared rhythm-part consisting of piano and drums.

Grade 9-10 “Eye of the Tiger”

This production was put together by the combined Grade 9/10 music class. Many of you will recognise this piece of music, entitled “Eye of the Tiger,” from the film, “Rocky 3”. The original version of “Eye of the Tiger” was recorded by the band Survivor in 1986. This instrumental version was arranged for the band by Gerald Sebesky.

Grade 11-12 “Mercy Mercy Mercy”

This production was recorded by the Grade 11/12 class. “Mercy, Mercy, Mercy” was a 1966 composition by jazz musician Joe Zawinul and was originally written for alto saxophonist Cannonball Adderly. Because of its strong rhythm and blues groove, it has often been recorded by many bands including the ’60’s pop group the Buckinghams and even more recently by Queen Latifah.
