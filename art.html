Grade 7/8 Visual Art

Grade 7 and 8 are exploratory years in visual art for students. They are introduced to the Elements of Art, get hands-on experience with different types of materials such as paint, oil pastel and markers. Students explore different subject matter and bring their own unique perspectives and creativity to their artwork. What you will find in the gallery is a collection of grade 7 & 8 artwork showcasing a number of different projects and excellent work of our students.
